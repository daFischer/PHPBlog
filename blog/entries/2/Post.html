A few things I forgot to mention as features to come:
  • Database in the backend. Right now I'm just loading from the filesystem. I'm not sure about how uploads should be done, though. Since many free webhosts don't have https (000webhost neither).
  • A search field on the left or in the header, where you can search for posts by tags.
  • Tags themselves! This is tied in with having the database running. I wouldn't try to simulate that one with just the filesystem.
  • A post history, where posts can be opened by publishing date. It would also be possible to view all posts of a chosen month or something like it.
  • I'll probably come up with something to fill the right side of the page as well. Newest Twitter posts? Favorite sites? There's so many options :D
  • A way of testing new posts offline, so nobody's able to sneak a peek at unpublished posts. This should be done pretty easily by creating a PageBuilder.js that copies PageBuilder.php. Keeping both in sync might become a hassle though. No solutions for that, yet.
It would probably be too early to talk about this blog's structure now, since there's a few issues waiting to be fixed. index.php is a mess and I'm not using Inversion of Control (explanation) anywhere. There's not many places for the latter to be used, yet, but it'll probably make things easier down the road.
